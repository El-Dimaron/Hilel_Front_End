Dark Fantasy Comic-Style Mini Stories


“Whispers in the Fog”

The village lay silent, cloaked in a fog so thick it swallowed the moonlight. Children claimed the mist carried voices, but tonight even the wolves were too afraid to howl. The air reeked of iron and wet earth, as though the ground itself had been freshly opened.


“The Broken Blade”

In the tavern’s ruins, a knight sat slumped against a charred beam, clutching a shattered sword. Its edge still dripped with shadows, not blood. Each blink brought back the battlefield—flames writhing like serpents, comrades falling with hollow eyes, and the sound of a hymn sung backward.


“The Last Door”

At the forest’s edge, an ancient door stood without walls, its frame twisted from roots and bone. Its surface pulsed faintly, as though it were alive. Behind it whispered promises of lost souls—voices of friends long buried, calling him closer. His hand trembled as it reached for the handle.
